How Can I Miss Her

一段关于暗恋、成长与释怀的故事

前言 / Introduction

This is a story about a boy and a girl. The boy is a student who has just entered college. He is ordinary and introverted, with no remarkable features. But one day, he met a girl who made his heart flutter. From then on, the boy's life changed quietly...

"有些故事，注定只能成为回忆；有些人，注定只能放在心里。"

This is just a small episode in life, but it has left a deep impression on me. I hope that through this story, everyone can find their own shadow in it.

初见 / First Meeting

The first time the boy saw the girl was in the library. At that time, the girl was sitting by the window, reading a book intently. The sunlight passed through the window and fell on her face, making her look particularly beautiful. The boy was immediately attracted by the girl's temperament. From that day on, the boy would go to the library to study every day, just to see the girl more.

心动的瞬间

阳光透过窗户，落在她的脸上，那一刻，我知道我心动了

深陷 / Deeply Fall

As time went on, the boy became more and more infatuated with the girl. He would pay attention to every move of the girl, secretly follow her after class, and even find out the girl's name and major from other people. But the boy was too timid to approach the girl. He could only watch the girl silently from a distance. This kind of secret love made the boy both happy and painful.

"暗恋是一种甜蜜的痛苦，你会因为她的一个微笑而开心一整天，也会因为她的一个冷漠而难过好几天。"

决定 / Decision

After thinking for a long time, the boy finally made a decision. He decided to confess his feelings to the girl. He wrote a long love letter, in which he expressed all his feelings for the girl. On a sunny afternoon, the boy mustered up his courage and walked up to the girl. But at the moment when he was about to speak, the boy saw the girl talking and laughing with another boy. The boy's courage was gone, and he silently walked away.

勇气的瞬间

当我准备表白时，却看到她和另一个男生有说有笑

黯伤 / Sorrow

After that, the boy was very sad. He felt that his world had collapsed. He began to doubt himself, thinking that he was not good enough, that he was not worthy of the girl. He often stayed up late, thinking about the girl. He even began to avoid the girl, because every time he saw the girl, his heart would ache.

"爱而不得，是人世间最痛苦的事情之一。当你深爱着一个人，却不能和她在一起，那种感觉就像是心被撕裂了一样。"

释怀 / Relief

After that, the boy confessed to the girl three times. Without exception, he was rejected. For a long time after that, the boy was trying to let her go. He tried very hard to forget her, but every time he saw her, he still felt very uncomfortable. But as time went on, he thought it over. The girl is someone he will never get, but she can be his White Moon Light forever. The boy had quietly let go of himself. After that, they became good friends, but only good friends. But the boy thought this is the best result.

最好的结果

她是永远得不到的白月光，却成了最好的朋友

这些是我心中她最美的样子，其实每一张都让我心动。每次看到这些照片，都会觉得很安心。

最珍贵的回忆

提升自我 / Improve oneself

These are just episodes in our lives, and there is no need to complain unnecessarily because of something we can't get. The only thing we need to do is self-discipline, strictly limit ourselves and return to the original state. Indulging in unnecessary sadness will only make us more and more decadent. Improving yourself is the only way.

应该做的

  • 严格自律，回归正轨
  • 持续提升自我能力
  • 制定并执行成长计划
  • 积极面对生活与挑战

不该做的

  • 沉溺于无意义的悲伤
  • 每日浑浑噩噩，虚度光阴
  • 因得不到而抱怨不休
  • 放弃自我成长与进步

唯有提升自己，才能拥有想要的一切

包括事业、爱情，以及更美好的人生

从现在开始提升自己

结语 / Concluding Remarks

This is just a sharing of my unforgettable personal experience, so please don't take it amiss. Thank you very much for listening today.

这只是一段难忘的个人经历分享，希望不会唐突。非常感谢大家今天的聆听。
